How to Use OrgTrace

Welcome to our transparent farm-to-table traceability platform. Whether you are a farmer recording a harvest, or a consumer checking a product's origin, the process is seamless and secure.

1
🦊

Connect Your Wallet

To interact with the blockchain securely, you need a Web3 wallet like MetaMask.

  • Click the "Connect MetaMask" button at the top right of the application.
  • Approve the connection prompt in your browser extension.
  • Once connected, your wallet address will be securely recognized by the app.
2
✍️

For Farmers: Add Products

Register your new organic batches securely onto the blockchain network.

  • Fill in the "Add New Product" form entirely (Product, Name, Location, Date).
  • Click "+ Save to Blockchain".
  • MetaMask will ask you to confirm a secure transaction. Accept it to permanently write the data.
  • Once confirmed, you will receive a unique Tracking ID.
3
🔍

For Consumers: Trace Origin

Instantly fetch immutable farm data directly from the smart contract.

  • Locate the Product ID on your packaging.
  • Enter the ID into the "Track Product Process" input.
  • Click "Initiate Tracking" to simulate the blockchain retrieval.
  • View exactly who farmed it, where, and when. You can also play the data out loud via the Audio Button!
