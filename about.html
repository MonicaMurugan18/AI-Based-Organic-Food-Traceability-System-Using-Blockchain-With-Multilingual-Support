About OrgTrace

Empowering the organic food supply chain with transparency, security, and trust through Web3 technologies.

🌍

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves to know exactly where their food comes from. Our mission is to bridge the gap between conscientious consumers and the dedicated farmers who work hard to produce genuine organic food. By eliminating intermediaries and verifying data on an immutable blockchain, we ensure authenticity at every step.

⛓️

Why Blockchain?

Traditional paper records and centralized databases are vulnerable to fraud and manipulation. By utilizing smart contracts, every harvest recorded on our platform is permanently etched into the blockchain. This means the data cannot be altered, providing a 100% verifiable and tamper-proof history of your agricultural products.

🤝

Who We Serve

We serve Farmers by giving them a platform to showcase their dedication to organic practices and connecting them directly with consumers who appreciate value. We serve Consumers by providing peace of mind through a transparent, verifiable ecosystem where every bite can be traced back to its root.
