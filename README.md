# AI-Based Organic Food Traceability System Using Blockchain With Multilingual Support

## Overview

This project is an AI-powered organic food traceability system that uses blockchain technology to securely track food products from farm to consumer. The system provides transparency, authenticity verification, and multilingual support, enabling users from different regions to access product information in their preferred language.

## Problem Statement

Consumers often struggle to verify whether food products are genuinely organic. Traditional record-keeping systems can be modified or manipulated, reducing trust and transparency in the supply chain.

## Solution

The proposed system stores organic food information on a blockchain, ensuring secure and tamper-proof records. Artificial Intelligence helps identify suspicious activities and quality issues, while multilingual support makes the platform accessible to a wider audience.

## Features

* Blockchain-based product registration
* Secure and immutable product records
* AI-powered monitoring and fraud detection
* Farmer and product information tracking
* Harvest date and location tracking
* Multilingual support for users
* Transparent supply chain management
* Product verification and traceability
